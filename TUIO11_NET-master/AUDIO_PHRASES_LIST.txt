========================================
NOTES FOR AUDIO RECORDING
========================================
- All phrases should be recorded in a clear, encouraging coaching voice
- Use a friendly, supportive tone
- Maintain consistent volume and quality across all recordings
- Record in a quiet environment without background noise
- Save as WAV or MP3 format
- Naming convention: phrase_001.wav, phrase_002.wav, etc.
- Place audio files in: TUIO11_NET-master/Audio/ folder
